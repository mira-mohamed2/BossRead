## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Open ReadFlow and tap **Register** on the login screen.
2. Enter your email address and a password (minimum 8 characters).
3. Confirm your password and tap **Create Account**.
4. Check your email for a verification link and confirm your account.

### Signing In

1. Enter your registered email and password on the login screen.
2. Tap **Sign In**.
3. You will be taken to the Library tab.

### Navigation

ReadFlow uses a bottom tab bar with five tabs:

| Tab | Icon | Description |
|-----|------|-------------|
| **Library** | Books icon | Your personal library of imported books and saved articles |
| **Discover** | Compass icon | Browse web novels from online sources (RoyalRoad, LightNovelPub) |
| **Browse** | Globe icon | Built-in web browser for saving articles from any website |
| **Stats** | Chart icon | Reading statistics and activity dashboard |
| **Settings** | Gear icon | Theme, font, and account settings |

## Library

The Library is your central hub for all content.

### Importing Books

1. Tap the **+** floating action button (bottom-right corner).
2. A file picker opens. Select an EPUB, PDF, or TXT file from your device.
3. The book appears in your library.

**Supported file formats:** EPUB, PDF, TXT

### Saving Articles

Articles are saved from the **Browse** tab. Once saved, they appear in your Library automatically.

### Searching Your Library

Use the search bar at the top of the Library to find items by title or author name.

### Filtering Content

Below the search bar, filter chips let you view:

- **All** — Every item in your library
- **Books** — Only books (EPUB, PDF, TXT)
- **Articles** — Only saved web articles
- **Bookmarks** — Only bookmarked/favorited items

### Deleting Items

Long-press on any library item to see options. Tap **Delete** to remove it.

### Reading Progress

Each library card shows a small progress bar and percentage indicating how far you have read.

### Read Time Estimates

Article cards display an estimated reading time (e.g., "5 min", "1h 20m") based on word count.

## Discover

The Discover tab lets you browse web novels from supported sources.

### Supported Sources

- **RoyalRoad** — English web fiction
- **LightNovelPub** — Light novels and web novels

### Browsing Novels

1. Select a source from the source selector at the top.
2. Use the tabs to switch between **Popular**, **Latest**, and **Search**.
3. Scroll through the list and tap any novel for details.

### Searching

1. Switch to the **Search** tab.
2. Type a query in the search bar and tap the search button.
3. Results appear from the selected source.

### Pagination

Scroll to the bottom of the list to automatically load more results.

---

## Browse

Browse is a built-in web browser for saving articles from any website.

### Navigating the Web

1. Type or paste a URL in the address bar at the top.
2. Tap Go (or press Enter).
3. Use the back/forward buttons in the toolbar to navigate.

### Saving an Article

1. Navigate to an article you want to read later.
2. Tap the **Save** button (download icon) in the browser toolbar.
3. ReadFlow extracts the article content and saves it to your Library.
4. You can now read it offline in the reader with customizable fonts and themes.

## Reader

The Reader provides a distraction-free reading experience.

### Opening Content

Tap any item in your Library to open it in the reader.

### Reader Controls

Tap anywhere in the middle of the screen to toggle the toolbar.

**Top toolbar:**
- **Back arrow** — Return to your library
- **Title** — Shows the current item's title
- **Bookmark icon** — Tap to bookmark/un-bookmark this item

**Bottom toolbar:**
- **-** / **+** buttons — Decrease or increase font size (12px to 32px)
- Current font size displayed between buttons

### Supported Content Types

| Type | Renderer |
|------|----------|
| EPUB | Full EPUB renderer with chapter navigation |
| PDF | Native PDF viewer with page navigation |
| TXT | Rendered as plain text with customizable styling |
| Articles | HTML content displayed with reader-friendly formatting |

### Reading Position

ReadFlow automatically saves your reading position. When you return to a book or article, it resumes where you left off.

## Text-to-Speech

ReadFlow includes a built-in text-to-speech engine for listening to your content.

### Starting TTS

The TTS mini player appears at the bottom of the reader. Tap the play button to start listening.

### Playback Controls

| Button | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **Skip Back** | Go to the previous sentence/chunk |
| **Play/Pause** | Start or pause playback |
| **Skip Forward** | Skip to the next sentence/chunk |
| **X** | Stop playback entirely |

### Adjusting TTS Settings

Tap the **options** icon (gear/settings icon) to expand the settings panel:

#### Speed
Choose from: **0.5x**, **0.75x**, **1.0x** (default), **1.25x**, **1.5x**, **2.0x**

#### Pitch
Choose from: **0.5**, **0.75**, **1.0** (default), **1.25**, **1.5**

#### Sleep Timer
Set a sleep timer to automatically stop playback after a duration:
**Off** (default), **5m**, **10m**, **15m**, **30m**, **60m**

## Reading Statistics

The Stats tab provides an overview of your reading activity.

### Stat Cards

Six cards display key metrics:

- **Total Reading** — Total time spent reading
- **Books** — Number of books in your library
- **Articles** — Number of articles in your library
- **Completed** — Items you have finished reading (100%)
- **Current Streak** — Consecutive days of reading
- **Longest Streak** — Your all-time best reading streak

### Activity Chart

A bar chart shows your reading activity for the last 14 days. Each bar represents one day.

### Most Read

A ranked list of your top 5 most-read items by progress percentage.

## Settings

### Theme

Choose from four themes:

| Theme | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Light** | Clean white background, dark text |
| **Dark** | Dark background, light text |
| **Sepia** | Warm paper-like tones, easy on the eyes |
| **AMOLED** | Pure black background for OLED screens |

### Font Size

Adjust the reader font size from 12px to 32px using the **-** / **+** buttons.

### Line Height

Adjust line spacing from 1.0 to 3.0 in 0.1 increments.

### Font Family

Choose from four font families:

| Font | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **System** | Your device default font |
| **Sans** | Clean sans-serif font |
| **Serif** | Traditional serif font |
| **OpenDyslexic** | Specialized font designed for readers with dyslexia |

### Account

- **Sign Out** — Signs you out of your account. Your data is synced to the cloud and will be available when you sign back in.

## Bookmarks

### Bookmarking an Item

1. Open any item in the reader.
2. Tap the **bookmark icon** in the top toolbar.
3. The icon fills in to indicate it is bookmarked.

### Viewing Bookmarks

1. Go to the Library tab.
2. Tap the **bookmark** filter chip.
3. Only bookmarked items are shown.

### Removing a Bookmark

1. Open the bookmarked item in the reader.
2. Tap the filled bookmark icon to remove the bookmark.

## Offline Reading

ReadFlow uses a local-first architecture. All your content is stored on your device and readable offline.

- **Books** are stored locally after import.
- **Articles** are saved with their full HTML content.
- **Reading progress** is tracked locally and syncs to the cloud when online.
- **Bookmarks and annotations** sync automatically when connectivity is restored.

## Tips & Tricks

1. **Quick theme switching** — Change themes in Settings; the entire app updates instantly.
2. **Sleep timer for bedtime reading** — Set the TTS sleep timer if you like to listen while falling asleep.
3. **Dyslexia-friendly reading** — Switch to the OpenDyslexic font and increase font size/line height for maximum readability.
4. **Progress tracking** — Check the Stats tab regularly to maintain your reading streak.
5. **Bulk reading** — Use the Browse tab to save multiple articles, then read them later in the Library.
6. **Long press to delete** — Long-press any library item for quick deletion.
